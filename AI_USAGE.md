# AI Usage

DeckProbe is developer / QA tooling — CDP probes, screenshot capture, and
diagnostics for Steam Deck plugins. In the spirit of community transparency,
this is a note on how AI is involved.

## How AI is used here

DeckProbe is **created and maintained with the help of AI**. As auxiliary
tooling — probes, helper scripts, glue code, runners, and docs — it's exactly
the kind of work where AI assistance is most useful, so it is used more here
than in the products this tooling supports. AI helps draft and iterate on:

- CDP session/probe helpers and diagnostic scripts,
- the screenshot / scenario runners,
- tests, CI, and documentation.

## What stays in human hands

Everything AI proposes is **validated, curated, and adjusted manually by the
maintainer** before it is used or merged — run against real hardware, checked,
trimmed, and corrected by hand. The maintainer is accountable for what lands
here. AI is a tool; it does not make the decisions.
